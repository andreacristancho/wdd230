Overview

Purpose

The Bucaramanga Chamber of Commerce, is the regional economic engine, leaders in convening and community advocates. It promotes business development through services for its members, fostering economic and community growth, and advocating for public policies that balance economic prosperity. Supporting small and medium-sized businesses, it boosts tourism and strengthens local commerce.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?

  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?

  • What has been the population growth in the area?

  • How can I acquire databases of the businesses I am interested in?

  • What training courses in alliance with universities are available for entrepreneurs?
